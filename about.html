The Product

Our Blitz kit offers a complete, on-the-go shoe care solution with premium features:

Cleaning Brush:

How to use

To clean your sneakers effectively, start by preparing the kit and wetting the brush in warm water. Add a few drops of the cleaning solution, then gently scrub your shoes to lift dirt and stains. Wipe away the foam with a microfiber cloth and let your sneakers air dry for a fresh finish.

Step 1:

Open the kit and take out the items. Pour warm water into the tray and dip the brush to wet the bristles.

Step 2:

Add a few drops of the cleaning solution onto the brush - just a small amount is enough.

Step 3:

Gently scrub your sneakers to remove dirt and stains. The solution will foam as it lifts away grime.

Step 4:

Wipe off the foam using the microfiber cloth, then leave your sneakers to dry.
