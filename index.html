FAQs

Have questions about Blitz? We’ve compiled the most common inquiries to help you make the best choice for your sneaker care needs.

How do I use Blitz?

Simply spray Blitz on your sneakers, let it sit for a minute, then wipe with a clean cloth for a spotless finish.

Is Blitz safe for all materials?

Yes! Blitz is specially formulated to be safe and effective on various materials, including leather, canvas, and synthetics.

How often should I clean my sneakers?

We recommend cleaning your sneakers every 2-4 weeks, or as needed, to maintain their appearance and extend their lifespan.

Can I use Blitz on other footwear?

Absolutely! Blitz works well on any type of footwear, including boots and casual shoes, to keep them looking fresh.

What if I have tough stains?

For tough stains, apply Blitz, let it sit for a few minutes, and gently scrub with a soft brush before wiping clean.
