Delivery Information

Order Processing : Once your order is placed, it will undergo a verification and approval process. This usually takes 1-2 business days. During this time, we ensure that your payment has been successfully processed, and your order details are correct.

Shipping Time : Once your order is processed, it will be dispatched from our warehouse. Delivery within India typically takes between 3-7 business days, depending on your location. Please note that during high-demand periods, such as holidays, delivery times may be slightly longer.

Shipping Charges :

  1. Free Shipping : All orders above ₹500 qualify for free shipping within India.
  2. Standard Shipping Fee: Orders below ₹500 will incur a standard shipping fee based on your location. This fee will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase

Order Tracking : After your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number to monitor your order’s progress and estimated delivery time. If you have any issues with tracking your order, our customer service team is always ready to assist.

Delivery Partners : We work with trusted courier partners to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders. If for any reason there is a delay, we will promptly notify you and work to resolve the issue.

International Shipping : At the moment, Blitz only ships within India. However, we are working on expanding our services internationally. Stay tuned for updates regarding international shipping options.

Important Notes :

  1. Please ensure your shipping details are accurate to avoid any delivery issues.
  2. If the delivery is delayed or an item is missing, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the estimated delivery date.
  3. Blitz is not responsible for any delays caused by customs or other external factors beyond our control.
